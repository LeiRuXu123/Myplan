About Us

Myplan is a personal finance management app that helps users effectively control their spending. It offers real-time spending reminders, making it easy for users to manage their expenses and providing personalized financial advice. Myplan is also integrated with users' bank accounts and e-wallets, making transaction monitoring a breeze.
